Unnat Jain

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at UIUC working with Alex Schwing and Svetlana Lazebnik. Previously, I completed my MS in Computer Science at UIUC. I am building AI agents that can collaborate and communicate in (virtual) visual worlds.

I have had the pleasure of interning with Rob Fergus at DeepMind (2021) and Kristen Grauman at Facebook AI Research (2019). I've spent two wonderful summers (2018, 2020) at Allen Institute for AI working with Luca Weihs, Ani Kembhavi, and Ali Farhadi.

Earlier, I graduated from IIT Kanpur where I worked with Vinay Namboodiri and Erik Learned-Miller.
